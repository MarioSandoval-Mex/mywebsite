Welcome!

I'm a PhD Student in Kessler's lab at Cornell University, conducting a study on sensitive plants in the Mimosa genus. My project will be focused on understanding the ecological and evolutionary implications of endogenous chemical signals (e.g., phytohormones) and their interactions with electrical signaling in plants of the genus Mimosa, from a phylogenetical and macroecological perspective. This knowledge is crucial for the general understanding of signal transduction in plants and thus for the potential utilization of induced plant responses in applied sciences (e.g., agriculture).
